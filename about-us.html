Why Do We Need Car Paint Nano Glass Coating

HYDROPHOBIC

This property repels water, so water will bead on the surface & slide off making it difficult for mud & grime to adhere to the surface, making washing easier. Also it prevents acidic fluid from bird droppings & dead bugs to etch the paint surface

WET LOOK

Having the ultimate shine is the pinnacle of importance to many car owners Customers have searched for an answer to keep their car shining HERE the Motorglaze Nano glass coating paint protection gives your car wet look for all seasons.

UV PROTECTION

protects the paint from being oxidised & fade when exposed in sun Increasing the life of the paint

DUST REPELLENT

Antistatic property makes sure that the dust doesn’t adhere to the paint making it easier to wipe & clean the paint surfaces.

SCRATCH RESISTANT

The hard glass coating ensures the paint to stay away from light scratches & swirl marks keeping it fresh n glossy.

FIRE RESISTANT

The Nano glass coating gives your car paint a proper glass coat making your car paint fire resistant.
